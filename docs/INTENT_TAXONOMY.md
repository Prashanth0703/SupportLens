# SupportLens Intent Taxonomy

## cancel_order

The customer explicitly asks to stop or cancel an existing order.

Example:
"Please cancel the order I placed yesterday."

## check_cancellation_fee

The customer asks whether cancelling an order has a cost or penalty.
They are not necessarily requesting cancellation.

Example:
"Will I be charged if I cancel now?"

## get_refund

The customer explicitly requests money back.

Example:
"The product arrived damaged and I want a refund."

## check_refund_policy

The customer asks about refund eligibility, time limits, or rules.

Example:
"How many days do I have to request a refund?"

## track_refund

A refund has already been initiated, and the customer asks about its status.

Example:
"My refund was approved last week but has not reached my account."
